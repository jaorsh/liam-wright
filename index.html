Welcome to Liam Wright Photography

about

Hey there! My name is Liam.

I picked up the passion for photography at the age of fourteen where I got my hands on my parents camera during family holidays. I would go out exploring new places and learning new things, and of course taking photos of the things I thought were most interesting.

Growing up in the countryside on a kiwifruit orchard and studying in the city, I feel I’ve had the best of both worlds. I enjoy being in the city, watching and discovering. But at the same time I enjoy being out in the open, being surrounded by nature and getting inspired by the little things that others may overlook.
As my discovery of photography went on, I moved further away from capturing landscapes, and starting photographing people on my adventures.

After this, I knew photography was what I wanted to be doing with my life.

So I decided to study a Bachelor of Applied Visual Imaging. Here, I found my calling. After shooting my first wedding, I was hooked. The adrenaline, the fun, the creative freedom and of course, meeting all sorts of interesting new people.

Since then, I’ve fully immersed myself in the wedding culture and I love it! My favourite part about weddings is finding those moments that make a person feel something when they look back at the photograph. Some kind of emotion, an interaction, or a moment that needs to be remembered. I’m based in the Bay of plenty, New Zealand and I love to travel my beautiful country. But where ever you may be in the world, I’d be happy to capture your special moments that you’ll treasure for the rest of your life.
 

If you want to know me a little more, feel free to give me a call or send me a message. To keep up with my travels and adventures, you can head over to my personal instagram to continue the visual experience.
